## Advanced AFK Farming Techniques

For many players, the grind can become taxing. This is where AFK (Away From Keyboard) farming comes into play. While the game encourages active participation, there are specific zones designed with passive resource generation in mind. Setting up your character in a safe zone with "Auto-Retaliate" enabled can allow you to farm lower-level mobs for gold and common materials while you are busy with other tasks.

To make the most of AFK farming, equip gear that provides "Health Regen" or "Life Steal." This ensures that your character can sustain themselves against minor chip damage from mobs. Additionally, using pets with "Auto-Loot" capabilities is essential; otherwise, the items dropped by your enemies will simply despawn before you return to your screen.

1. **Find a High-Density Spawn:** Look for corners where mobs naturally congregate.
2. **Equip Sustain Gear:** Focus on defense over pure offense to survive long durations.
3. **Use a Loot Pet:** Ensure every drop is collected automatically.
4. **Set a Timer:** Check back every 60 minutes to clear your inventory and sell junk.

As you progress through the game, your farming location must change to keep pace with your level.
