## The Secret of the Pity System

One of the most misunderstood mechanics in the game is the Pity System. For many players, securing rare **slime seas material drops** feels like an endless cycle of RNG. However, the system is designed with a safety net that activates on your 25th boss kill. While most players assume they have to kill the same high-level boss 25 times to trigger this guarantee, there is a much more efficient way to manipulate this mechanic.

By utilizing "trash" bosses, you can prime your pity counter. This allows you to guarantee a high-tier drop on your first attempt at a difficult, summoned boss. This is particularly vital for materials like the Slime Core, which typically only has a 0.8% base drop rate. Without using the pity system, you could theoretically go hundreds of runs without seeing a single core.

### How to Trigger the Pity Exploit

Follow these specific steps to ensure your pity is active before you spend your valuable summoning materials:

1.  **Travel to Boss Island:** Locate the weakest boss available in the current region. In the current 2026 meta, this is typically the Saber boss.
2.  **Enable Auto-Summon:** Use the auto-summon feature to keep the boss spawning immediately after death.
3.  **Kill Count Tracking:** You must kill the weak boss exactly **24 times**. Do not kill the 25th, as this will consume the pity on a low-value target.
4.  **Swap Targets:** Once your counter is at 24, head immediately to the boss that drops the rare **slime seas material drops** you actually need.
5.  **Claim Your Reward:** Your next kill on the high-level boss will trigger the pity system, significantly increasing the likelihood of receiving the rarest item in its loot table.
