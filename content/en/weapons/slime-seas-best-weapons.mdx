### Katana Mastery: The PvE King
For players who spend their time hunting bosses or farming slimes, Katana Mastery is the undisputed meta. Its primary advantage lies in its attack speed. The Katana's skills are designed to chain together, allowing for a fluid combat style that keeps you mobile while dealing consistent damage.

### Scythe Mastery: The PvP Sweat’s Choice
If you find yourself frequently engaged in combat with other players, the Scythe is your best friend. In the 2026 competitive landscape, crowd control is everything. The Scythe Mastery is loaded with "grab" moves. These abilities physically displace your opponent and apply a brief stun, leaving them completely vulnerable to follow-up combos. A skilled Scythe user can effectively "infinite combo" an unprepared player, making it one of the most feared **slime seas best weapons** in the arena.

> **💡 Expert Tip:** When using the Scythe in PvP, always initiate with a grab move. If the grab connects, your opponent cannot use their escape abilities for 1.5 seconds, giving you a window for a full skill rotation.

## Deep Dive: The Rimuru Sword Mastery

One of the most sought-after weapons in the game is the Rimuru Sword. To begin this journey, you must travel to Slime Island and locate the Rimmeiru Master NPC, who is situated at the leftmost edge of the island.

To unlock the final F-move and the coveted 30% damage boost, you must complete a series of rigorous challenges. This is not for the faint of heart, but the power spike is well worth the effort.

## Efficient Farming: The Road to 10,000 Kills

The most daunting part of mastering the **slime seas best weapons** is often the 10,000 NPC kill requirement. Trying to hit this number through natural progression can take weeks. However, veteran players utilize specific "AFK farming" locations to hit this milestone in a fraction of the time.

The premier location for this in 2026 is **Soul Society**. Specifically, you want to target the Quincy mobs. These NPCs spawn in tight clusters and have relatively low health compared to their experience yield. 

### How to AFK Farm Masteries:
1. **Equip your target weapon:** You only gain mastery for the weapon you currently have active.
2. **Travel to Soul Society:** Locate the area where Quincy mobs congregate.
3. **Use an Auto-Clicker:** Set your character to use basic attacks or low-cooldown AOE moves.
4. **Positioning:** Stand in the center of at least three spawn points to ensure constant combat.

> **⚠️ Warning:** While AFK farming is a common practice, ensure you are in a private server or a safe zone to avoid being killed by "bounty hunters" who prowl popular farming spots.

## Great Sword and Berserker Stance

The Great Sword remains a staple for players who prefer a "tanky" playstyle. Its "E" move is legendary for its massive AOE, which can clear an entire wave of mobs in a single strike. However, the real power of the Great Sword is unlocked via the **Berserker Mastery**.

Berserker Mastery is a unique stance that can be toggled on. It significantly increases your attack power and changes your move set to be more aggressive. Note that this is currently tied to a Game Pass, making it a "premium" option. For players willing to invest, the Berserker stance elevates the Great Sword from a reliable tool to one of the **slime seas best weapons** for high-level dungeon clearing.

## FAQ

**Q: What are the slime seas best weapons for beginner players?**

A: To unlock the 30% damage boost, you must fully complete the weapon's mastery track. For specialized weapons like the Rimuru Sword, this involves defeating specific bosses on Hard/Extreme difficulty and reaching 10,000 total kills with that weapon equipped.

**Q: Is the Berserker Mastery worth the Game Pass cost?**

**Q: Where is the best place to farm 10,000 kills quickly?**
